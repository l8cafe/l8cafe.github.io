About Us

L8 Café is a great place to chill and surf the net; to catch up with work out of the office or to simply enjoy the life and our great food meeting friends.

The L8 idea was born when a young Serbian duo first started selling gourmet sandwiches and tasty soups at a local market stall in Rosebank.

Since then the concept has flourished and expanded into what is now L8 Cafe in the 44 Stanley precinct, one of Jozi’s sneaky surprises tucked away in Milpark. Stop by for a coffee fix, a superb lunch, a real beer on tap or just quick snack under the olive trees in the sunny main yard.

L8 Cafe has the perfect atmosphere to catch up on your daily read, check your emails, get your creative juices flowing or just to meet up with friends for one of Jozi’s best burgers or fresh salads.
